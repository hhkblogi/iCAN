# iCAN++ Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

iCAN++ is an iPadOS app for monitoring and testing CAN bus hardware through supported USB CAN adapters.

## Data Collection

iCAN++ does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or account sign-in. It does not contact a remote server as part of normal operation.

## Local Hardware Data

iCAN++ reads CAN bus frames and USB adapter status from hardware connected to the user's iPad. This data is processed locally on the device for dashboard display, message logging, diagnostics, and testing. It is not sent to the developer or to any third party by the app.

## Permissions and DriverKit

iCAN++ includes a DriverKit extension used to communicate with supported USB CAN adapters. Users may need to approve the driver in iPadOS Settings before using connected hardware.
